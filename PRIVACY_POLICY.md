# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how EurekaNow ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and discloses personal data when customers and users access our service desk platform (the "Service").

## 1. Scope

This policy applies to data processed through the EurekaNow web application, related support channels, and associated infrastructure.

It does not apply to third-party websites or services that we do not control.

## 2. Roles

Depending on context:

- Customer is typically the data controller for data entered into tickets, users, comments, and knowledge base content.
- We typically act as a data processor/service provider handling data on Customer's instructions.
- We may act as a controller for account administration, billing, security, and legal compliance data.

## 3. Data We Collect

### 3.1 Account and Identity Data

- Name
- Email address
- Organization and team identifiers
- Role and permission metadata
- Login and authentication details (as configured)

### 3.2 Service Data (Customer Data)

- Ticket content, categories, status, assignees, timestamps, comments, tags
- Knowledge base articles and metadata
- Team, organization, and workflow settings
- Post-incident review details

### 3.3 Device and Usage Data

- Browser type and version
- IP address and approximate location derived from IP
- Device and operating system data
- Log events and telemetry for reliability and security

### 3.4 Support and Communications Data

- Messages sent to support
- Feedback and feature requests

## 4. How We Use Data

We use personal data to:

- Provide, maintain, and improve the Service
- Authenticate users and manage access
- Process and display tickets, comments, workflows, and dashboards
- Monitor performance, debug issues, and secure the platform
- Respond to support requests
- Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell personal data.

## 5. Legal Bases (where applicable)

Depending on your jurisdiction, we process data based on:

- Contract necessity
- Legitimate interests (for security, product improvement, fraud prevention)
- Legal obligations
- Consent, where required

## 6. Data Sharing

We may share data with:

- Infrastructure and hosting providers (for example database, cloud, analytics, and support tooling)
- Professional advisors (legal, audit, compliance)
- Authorities where required by law

We require processors/sub-processors to protect data and use it only for authorized purposes.

## 7. International Transfers

Data may be processed in countries other than your own. Where required, we implement appropriate transfer safeguards.

## 8. Data Retention

We retain data for as long as needed to provide the Service and fulfill legal, accounting, or security obligations.

Retention periods may vary by data category and customer agreement.

## 9. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards, including access controls and monitoring, to protect personal data.

No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

## 10. Your Rights

Depending on applicable law, individuals may have rights to:

- Access personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete data
- Restrict or object to processing
- Data portability
- Withdraw consent (where processing is based on consent)

For data controlled by a Customer, please contact your organization first. We will assist Customers in responding to valid requests.

## 11. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We may use cookies or similar technologies for session management, authentication, preferences, and analytics.

Where required, users can manage cookie preferences through browser settings or consent tools.

## 12. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 13 (or higher age where required by local law), and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## 13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through reasonable channels.
