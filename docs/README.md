## Compliance Standards Covered

- **UK GDPR** / Data Protection Act 2018 (ICO)
- **ISO 27001:2022** — alignment guidance (not yet certified)
- **Cyber Essentials** — self-assessment checklist
- **PCI DSS v4.0** — SAQ A (card data never touches our servers)
- **WCAG 2.1 Level AA** — web accessibility
- **NIS2 Directive** — where applicable to UK operations

## Quick Reference — Legal Basis for Processing

| Processing Activity | Legal Basis (UK GDPR Art. 6) |
|---|---|
| Account registration & login | Contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) |
| Service delivery (tickets, KB, etc.) | Contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) |
| Billing & invoicing | Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)) |
| Marketing communications | Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) |
| Security logging & audit trails | Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)) |
| Analytics & product improvement | Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)) |
